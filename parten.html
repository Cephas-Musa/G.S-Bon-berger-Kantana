Our Development Journey

Since its creation, Good Shepherd Kanzana has progressively developed from a new educational initiative into an institution combining academic learning with sustainable development projects that support both the school and the surrounding community.

School Foundation

Good Shepherd Kanzana was founded with the mission of providing quality education grounded in discipline, knowledge and moral values. The institution started with a strong commitment to serve the local community and prepare students for the future.

Academic Development

The school gradually expanded its academic activities by opening classrooms, recruiting qualified teachers and welcoming new students. Continuous improvement of teaching resources remains a priority.

Practical Training for Students

In order to combine theory and practice, the school introduced agricultural and livestock activities. These initiatives allow students to gain practical skills while supporting the institution’s sustainability.

Strategic Investment Projects

Today, Good Shepherd Kanzana is implementing structured agricultural and livestock investment programs including crop production, dairy farming and poultry units. These projects strengthen food security, create income for the school and offer practical vocational training opportunities for students.
